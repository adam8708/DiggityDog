My name is Sasha and I live in Blackpool, Lancashire

Hi, I'm Sasha! I'm 18 months old and living with my amazing foster mum in Blackpool, Lancashire. They called me Sasha because when they found me they thought I was a girl at first then quickly realised I am a boy! But the name's stuck now.. I love walking and cuddles with my family. I don't mind being alone, but I can be nervous with new people and especially bigger dogs. If you slowly tell me it's ok, I'm fine with them after a while. My mum is teaching me to live happily with her cat. I would love a family to call my own, without young children as I like to take things slow. It would be great if I was an only pet as I'm still very nervous, but I could live with a confident dog, though preferably not one that's too much bigger than me!

Can you give me a forever home?
