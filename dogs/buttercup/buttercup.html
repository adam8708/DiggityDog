My name is Buttercup and I live in Conwy, North Wales

Hi, I'm Buttercup, and I'm a small (7kg) sized 2 year old girl living in foster in Conwy, North Wales. My foster mummy has been amazing and helping me come out of my shell, but I'm still scared sometimes. I was saved from a public shelter in Codlea, Romania, and sometimes I forget that I'm safe now and try to hide. I love my crate and bed, but doorways make me nervous. I can walk okay on the harness, but not very far. I know not to pee and poo inside, and will go out to the garden for it. My foster mum thinks that I would love to live with a confident dog who can really guide me. My favourite thing in the whole world is cheese so give me some cheese and I will follow your lead! Please adopt me!
