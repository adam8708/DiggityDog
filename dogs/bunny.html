My name is Bunny and I live in South Lanarkshire, Scotland

Hi, I'm Bunny and I'm a 5 years old. I'm a medium sized girl - about the size of a labrador. I didn't have the best start in life - I was saved from a public shelter in Romania, but I had mange badly which meant I wasn't fit to travel for a long time. Lucky for me, my foster mum was patient and waited for me to recover until I could come into her home. At my foster mum's home, I settled in not too badly considering the trauma of living in a public shelter and then a rescue for a long time! I am super friendly and love hooman contact. My foster mum has 3 other dogs and it took me a while to settle in with them and find my place within the home but now I'm so very comfortable. I can sleep in a crate or on a doggy bed, but I prefer the safety of the crate at night. When I first arrived, I did cry a bit because I as scared but that soon went away and I started to feel happy again. I like to lie outside as that is what I have been used to - old habits die hard! I love to walk on the lead, and I float like a cloud (so my foster mum says). I love food, any food.. and I dance when you put it down to me. I am a proper lady, I know how it's important to go to the correct toilet (the one in the garden!). Please give me a forever home - I will be your loyal 4-legged friend!
