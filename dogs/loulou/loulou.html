My name is Lou Lou and I live in Gravesend, Kent

Lou Lou is a super friendly 3 year old small sized girl who is looking for her forever home!

She was saved with her sister, Lina, and was brought into foster in September in the UK as they were really struggling with living in kennels in Romania

They were adopted together, but unfortunately their adopter was struggling to cope with both girls as Lou Lou was so desperate to be outdoors all the time. Lou Lou now lives in a foster home in Gravesend, Kent, and we hope that her next home will be her forever home!

She's ideally looking for a family home with a garden as she loves the outdoors. She adores children so would happily live in a home with children, and is fantastic with other pets.

Please give her a forever home!
