My name is Milo and I live in Lincolnshire

My name is Milo! I’m 8 months old and in foster with my brother Cooper’s adoptive family. We get along great! I struggle a lot with dermodex, and it’s affected the right side of my face as a result, but it doesn’t make a difference to who I am. I still love having fun and playing with my brother. Cooper has been in the UK longer than me so he’s a lot more confident, but I’m still getting there. I’m really scared of men, and need time to get used to you. I’m scared of loud noises and fast movements, but I’m sure I’ll start to learn soon. I would love a quiet home to call my own! I live in Lincolnshire and would prefer for any potential family to be able meet me before adopting so I can get to know you. Please adopt me!
