Scapa, 5-7 years old German Shepherd, in foster DEREHAM, NORFOLK

Scapa is a German Shepherd, her passport states her to be 8 years old but we think she is nearer 5 or 6.

Scapa was rescued from Codlea public shelter, where she was struggling with being confined and was really stressed. She is absolutely made for family life, she is typical of her breed is devoted to those she loves. Being alone and scared in the shelter must have been very hard for her.

Since arriving in the UK, Scapa has slotted perfectly into her foster home. She gets on very well with the other dogs she lives with, is great with the children and is even good with the free range chickens. She is clean indoors, travels well in a car and can be left for a few hours with no issues.

Scapa is lovely to walk and is thrilled to meet all the people and dogs she comes across. She is also very good off-lead with excellent recall.

Scapa would suit a family who are around most of the time as she just thrives on being with her people. She would love to be at the heart of everything that is going on and her easygoing, friendly temperament means that you can take her anywhere and know that she will be an absolute star.
