My name is Scapa and I live in Dereham, Norfolk

I'm Scapa and I'm a German Shepherd living with my foster family in Dereham, Norfolk. My passport says I'm 8 years old, but everyone thinks I'm closer to 5 or 6. I used to live in this awful shelter in Codlea. It was a public shelter and I was treated awfully. I hated the kennels there, and really struggled in the kennels at the shelter where I saved too. I got so stressed that my skin reacted really badly.

Fortunately, Katy offered me a home and I'm on the mend now. I love living in a real family, and will always be loyal to you. I have so much space and freedom, and love hanging out with the other dogs at home and with the children there too. I even get to hang out with their free range chickens!

I love to walk, and meeting everyone and every dog on our walks. I know the toilet is outside, and I know that when I'm on my own at home that you'll be coming back.

However, I'd really like a home where someone is around most of the time as I just want to be around people, be it in the home or outdoors, I just want to be there with you! Please adopt me!
